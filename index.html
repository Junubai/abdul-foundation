About Us

Our Story

The Abdul Foundation was established in 2021, in the aftermath of the COVID-19 pandemic, as a small community-based initiative. Today, it has grown into a recognized and registered organization implementing diverse programs across Uganda.

Our work focuses on providing free primary education, addressing hunger, and promoting health and sanitation awareness. Through these initiatives, we have positively impacted countless lives and transformed many families and communities.

Guided by hope, love, and dedication, we remain committed to creating lasting change—one life at a time.

The Abdul Foundation seeks to provide Free Quality Education, Love, Hope and Care to the Orphaned and Abandoned children of Uganda. There are over 40 million people living in Uganda, More than 10 million are orphaned children.

Natural factors as well as diseases [AIDs, Malaria, Typhoid among others] have wiped out parents, leaving thousands of children with no Parental Love and Care, No education and with little HOPE. We currently fully support (25 kids & 12 youths) and extend support to more than 300 kids aged between 3 to 15 years Old and we make sure all their basic needs are met at their different destinations though most of the children face psychological and social problems as they grow and they are also vulnerable to sexual harassments and exploitation due to lack of care and protection that's why we want to create a safe SHELTER/ HOME for all.

The Abdul Foundation is a faith and Donation based organization. Our Mission, Vision and Objectives are in line with God that our children will grow up to be men and women who know the love of their creator in their respective religions.

The Abdul Foundation has for years supported, strengthened vulnerable families to improve their wellbeing, set up a temporary HOME for Kids of 2 to 15 years old and 8 staff members who look after these children. At 50$ a month you can feed an orphan. Your Generous and Faithful Support will help us achieve our Mission to transform the lives of these Children.

Our Initiatives

Quality Education

Education is the most powerful yet expensive tool in breaking the poverty cycle and empowering future generations. We offer quality education to orphaned children and vulnerable youths (25 in primary level & 12 in vocational training) as well as extending support [books, pencils, bags] to more than 300 children in different destinations. At 80$ a term you can sponsor a primary level student and at 120$ a semester you can sponsor a vocational level student.

Zero Hunger

An empty belly leads to an empty mind, unhealthy body and unhappy thoughts. High living costs, corruption and embezzlement are key factors to an escalating Malnutrition that has led to stunted growth in many vulnerable orphaned children across Uganda. At 50$ a month you can help feed an orphan.

Learn More

Community Economic Growth

Empowerment is key to lifting the lives and families out of poverty. Through our Community outreaches we spare time and share entrepreneurial knowledge, skills with vulnerable communities to help build monetary support and sustainability. Craft making, Drawing & Painting are taught as well as providing materials to help them grow their small businesses.

Learn More

Clean water and sanitation

Natural factors as well as poor sanitation, garbage disposal, pit latrines have contaminated the water flows. This has led to inadequate access to clean drinking water. By implementing water conservation policies & projects like borehole construction, water harvesting and water treatment can help improve the quality of water in the communities.

Learn More

Good Health and well being

Access to healthcare is a daily challenge each year that has claimed many lives paving way to an increasing number of orphaned children. Through health awareness campaigns and education programs [Aids program], Mobile clinics and medical camps, can help reduce on the limited access to health facilities, medical supplies which can put a positive impact on the health of the vulnerable children, Elderly people and the communities as a whole.

Our Mission

The Abdul Foundation’s mission is to provide love and care, quality education for orphaned children and vulnerable youth, access to clean water, and essential medical care for healthier communities. We also extend economic support to poor families to improve their well-being, reduce the rising number of orphans, and create safe shelters and homes for orphaned children.
